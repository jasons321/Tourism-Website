Legendary places.

Jakarta

Kebon Sirih fried rice

Famous for their lamb fried rice, this has place is regarded as one of the best fried rice stalls in Jakarta.

Jl. Danau Permai no. 12B Pamulang 10310

Soto Betawi in Pondok Pinang

Traditional soup consisting of beef and other ingredients, soto betawi is a local favourite and this place does it best!

Jl Pondok Pinang Raya No 21

Gado-gado Bonbin

Bonbin has been serving gado-gado (mixed vegetable with peanut sauce) for 50 years, a must-try when you visit Jakarta.

Jl. Cikini IV 1330

Bubur Ayam Barito

Fancy a warm porridge covered in various toppings to start off your morning? This delicious dish will leave you wanting a second bowl.

Jl. Gandaria Tengah 3 Kramat Pela

Bali

Babi Guling Bu Oka

Babi guling is a staple of balinese cuisine where a variety of toppings are complemented with rice, with a side of crispy pork skin. Bu Oka is renowned for this dish.

Jalan Tegal Sari No. 2, Ubud

Sate Babi Bawah Pohon

One of the best places in Bali to eat grilled skewered pork. The charred bits paired with a hint of sweetness makes this a perfect dish to enjoy after a long day of adventures.

Jl. Dewi Sri Campuhan I, Legian

Radja Seafood Cafe

Looking to relax, enjoy the sound of waves crashing ashore while eating delicious seafood? Radja Seafood cafe is the perfect place to have the best dinner experience of your lifetime.

Jl. Four Season, Jimbaran 80364

Warung Wardani

Rice paired with a variety of dishes of your choosing, Warung Wardani is a must-try in Bali.

Jl. Yudistira no. 2, Denpasar

Yogyakarta

Gudeg Mbah Lindu

This restaurant has been selling gudeg for over 80 years. Gudeg is a rice dish paired with toppings such as marinated eggs, chicken, and sambal sauce for a bit of a kick.

Jl. Sosrowijayan Sosromenduran, Gedong Tengen

Bakmi Jowo Mbah Gito

Often called Java noodle, mie jowo is a traditional noodle dish consisting of yellow noodle and a variety of other spices. Mbah Gito is the go-to place for trying this dish.

Jl. Nyi Ageng Nis No.9, Rejowinangun

Tiwul Ayu Mbok Sum

A unique traditional Yogyakarta dessert made from cassava. Make sure to pair it with some tea.

Jl. Mangunan Km 4,5

Bakpia Kurnia Sari

Popular pastry that contains different types of fillings, don't miss out!

Jl. Glagahsari no. 91C
